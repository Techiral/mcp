Hunter x Hunter is a Japanese manga series written and illustrated by Yoshihiro Togashi. It follows the story of Gon Freecss, a young boy who discovers that his father, who he was told was dead, is actually a world-renowned Hunter, a licensed professional who specializes in fantastical pursuits such as locating rare or unidentified animal species, treasure hunting, surveying unexplored enclaves, or hunting down individuals. Gon leaves his home on Whale Island to become a Hunter and eventually find his father.
